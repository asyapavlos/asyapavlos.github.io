About Me

Collect, process, analyse and present data — from supporting everyday business decisions to driving global change.

I am a Business Intelligence Analyst with experience bridging business requirements and technical solutions. My background spans business processes, commercial departments, and IT projects. I design semantic data models, create interactive dashboards, and ensure KPI governance for better decision-making.

Key skills include Power BI, DAX, SQL, data modeling (Kimball star schema, SCD), and requirements engineering (BRDs, BPMN). I have worked on reporting automation, assortment optimization, and customer insights projects, helping organizations reduce reporting cycles and adopt data-driven strategies.

Fluent in German (C1), English (C2), and Russian, I enjoy working in international environments, collaborating with stakeholders, and translating data into clear insights that bring measurable business impact.
