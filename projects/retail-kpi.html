Retail KPI Dashboard & Semantic Model

Power BI · DAX · SQL · Star schema · RLS

Objective

Provide executives and merchandising teams with a single source of truth for sales, margin, and customer trends, enabling faster decision-making and consistent KPI definitions.

Key Features

  • Executive dashboard summarizing sales, margin %, and trend alerts.
  • Customer detail page with segmentation, CLV proxy, and cohort analysis.
  • Product view highlighting mix, margin contribution, and top/bottom performers.
  • Drill-through paths from executive KPIs down to customer or product level.
  • Row-level security by region, ensuring users see only relevant data.

Design Highlights

  • Clear KPI layer in the semantic model ensures one version of the truth.
  • Consistent design language across pages, improving user adoption.
  • Interactive filters and drill-through improve usability for both executives and analysts.

Technical Approach

  • Modeling: Kimball-style star schema with conformed Date, Customer, Product, and Region dimensions.
  • Measures: DAX KPIs for sales, margin, CLV proxy, and comparative YoY metrics.
  • Performance: Query folding and optimized relationships for smooth interaction.
  • Governance: Data dictionary documenting KPIs, columns, and business definitions.

Business Impact

  • Reduced reporting cycle by 30% through self-service dashboards.
  • Improved executive confidence by providing consistent KPIs across regions and teams.
  • Increased adoption of BI tools across merchandising and sales departments.
