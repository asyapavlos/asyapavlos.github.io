Bellabeat – Fitness Data Case Study

In this project I analyzed smart device usage data to discover insights about activity, sleep, and wellness habits. The goal was to understand user behavior and provide recommendations that can support Bellabeat’s product strategy and marketing decisions.

The study covers data cleaning, exploration, visualization, and generation of actionable business insights.
