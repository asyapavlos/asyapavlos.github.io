Performance Dashboard

P&L, cashflow, and variance analysis. Calculation groups, aggregations, and RLS for exec-ready performance and security.

Overview

Executive dashboard covering Profit & Loss, cash and forecast vs actuals. Dimensional model with conformed Date, Org, and Product; semantic layer exposes clean measures and calc groups.

Highlights

  • Aggregation tables + calculation groups → fast slice & drill
  • Incremental refresh and partitions for large fact tables
  • RLS by org unit; governed KPI dictionary for one version of truth

Result

Reduced dashboard load time from ~12s to <2s, standardized KPI definitions across finance and sales, and enabled secure access by role.
