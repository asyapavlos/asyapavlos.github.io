Gross Profit Performance Dashboard

Power BI · DAX · SQL · Data Modeling

Objective

Provide executives and regional managers with a single source of truth on gross profit performance, enabling quick detection of underperforming countries, product categories, and customer accounts.

Key Features

  • KPI cards highlight gross profit (YTD), variance vs. prior year, and margin % at a glance.
  • Tree map ranks the bottom 10 countries by YTD performance, making loss-driving markets immediately visible.
  • Variance waterfall explains month-by-month profit drivers (increases/decreases).
  • Stacked column with line compares product types across months vs. PYTD trend.
  • Scatterplot plots GP% vs. value by account, exposing low-margin, high-volume customers.

Design Highlights

  • Consistent color semantics: green = increase, red = decrease, blue = total.
  • Layered visuals (bar + line, scatter + reference lines) provide multi-dimensional insight.
  • Drill-down paths by country → month → product → account enable flexible exploration.

Technical Approach

  • Modeling: Kimball-style star schema with conformed dimensions (Date, Country, Product, Account).
  • Measures: DAX for YTD, PYTD, and variance (absolute & % change).
  • Performance: Query folding, optimized relationships, VertiPaq compression.
  • Governance: Standardized KPI definitions across Finance and Sales for “one version of the truth.”

Business Impact

  • Enabled executives to identify underperforming markets 2 weeks earlier than with static reports.
  • Reduced monthly reporting cycle from 5 days to 2 days.
  • Improved pricing and account strategy decisions by exposing low-margin/high-volume accounts.
